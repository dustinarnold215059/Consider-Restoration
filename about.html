About Christopher Rembisz, LMT

Licensed Massage Therapist & Certified Personal Trainer

Christopher completed his 625-hour massage program in 2017 and has been dedicated to ongoing education in neurology and advanced bodywork techniques. His unique approach combines traditional massage therapy with applied neurology and corrective exercise to address the root causes of pain and dysfunction.

Specializing in pain management, postural assessment, and neurology-focused bodywork, Christopher believes that "pain shouldn't be your normal" and works with each client to restore their body's natural function and movement patterns.

Specialties:

  • Applied Neurology & Bodywork
  • Pain Management & Relief
  • Postural Assessment & Correction
  • Prenatal Massage
  • Thai-Stretch Fusion Techniques

My Philosophy

Every client deserves personalized care that addresses their unique needs. I believe in taking the time to understand your specific concerns and creating a treatment plan that works for your body and lifestyle. Through continuing education and specialized training, I stay current with the latest techniques in therapeutic bodywork.
